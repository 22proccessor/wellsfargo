Senior housing financing, from construction to permanent

To make your senior care facility a reality, many pieces need to come together. We specialize in bringing together construction financing, permanent loan programs, and more, to help experienced healthcare operators and owners reach their financial goals.

Customers

Non-profit and for-profit organizations including operators/owners, private equity groups, and REITs with strong track records within the industry

Loan solutions

  • Construction loans 
  • Bridge and mini-perms 
  • Portfolio-level financings 
  • Permanent loan programs 
  • Construction to permanent loans 
  • Repositioning/rehab financing 
  • Subscription lines 
  • Derivatives
  • Revolving lines of credit 
  • Cash flow loans 
  • Tax-exempt financing 
  • Bond credit enhancements

Loan Size

$10+ million; we consider smaller amounts on a case-by-case basis.

Eligible projects

  • Age-restricted apartments
  • Independent living (IL)
  • Assisted living (AL)
  • Memory care (MC)
  • Alzheimer’s (ALZ)
  • Skilled nursing facilities (SNF)
