Supporting your business in Latin America and abroad

Customized financial solutions and long-term relationships that can help you achieve your goals

For companies with operations or buyer/supplier relationships in Latin America, Wells Fargo can provide a broad range of services directly, on a cross-border basis, or through our partner networks.

Our services

Wells Fargo provides the guidance, support and expertise to help you manage risk and take advantage of opportunities in Latin America. Capabilities include:

  • Channel finance
  • Financing
  • Foreign exchange
  • Trade services
  • Treasury management

Working with you in Latin America

Our teams in the U.S. and across the globe work together to deliver tailored solutions and extensive expertise for both your parent and subsidiary locations. We take the time to understand your business and leverage the strength and stability of Wells Fargo to deliver exceptional service to help you succeed globally.

We also offer a wide range of services for financial institutions.

Wells Fargo does not have offices outside of the U.S. that provide services to retail or small business customers. For assistance with your accounts when traveling internationally, refer to our international access codes for phone numbers, or visit our travel resource center.

Wells Fargo conducts business outside the U.S. through various companies, including subsidiaries and affiliates. All products and services may not be available in all countries. Each situation needs to be evaluated individually and is subject to local regulatory requirements.
