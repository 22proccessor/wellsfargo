How to switch to Wells Fargo

  • Print

We’ll help you get started banking with Wells Fargo

Make the switch in three simple steps.

Step 1: Fund your new account

Make a check deposit with the Wells Fargo Mobile®  app, through Direct Deposit, or in person

  • Set up Direct Deposit
  • Pre-filled Direct Deposit form

Step 2: Move your payments

Review your old account statement for automatic withdrawals or payments

Step 3: Close your old account

Once scheduled payments, checks, or transactions have cleared, move any remaining balance to Wells Fargo, and close your old account
