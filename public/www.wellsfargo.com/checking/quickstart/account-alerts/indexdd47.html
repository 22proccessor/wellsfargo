Get Account Alerts

  • Print

Help stay on top of your account balance and transactions via text or email alerts

Set up alerts when your account balance falls below an amount you set, purchases go over a limit you specify, and more.

Alerts can give you the status of your accounts which can help you monitor your account against fraud and keep you on top of your spending.

Choose to get alerts sent to your email or mobile phone as text messages. In some cases, alerts can be sent via text and push notifications through the mobile app.

To set up alerts, enroll in or sign on to Wells Fargo Online® and access alerts.
