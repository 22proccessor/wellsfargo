Routing Numbers and Account Numbers

  • Print

Routing numbers and account numbers are available in Wells Fargo Online®. Take the tour to learn more.

To help us provide you with the information you're looking for, please answer the following question(s):

Are you looking for a routing number or an account number?
     

For your security, we don't display account numbers here. See below for help locating your account and routing numbers.

View online

To find your full account and routing numbers in your account online, simply sign on to Wells Fargo Online® and select the account you’re interested in. Then, follow these instructions:

Account number

To access your full account number, select Account and the 4 numbers directly below the account name.


Routing number

To access your routing number, select Routing numbers directly below the account name and after the account number.


View your account statement

Your account number is located at the top of your online or paper statement.

Look at a check

Your account number is located along the bottom of your check, to the right of the routing number and to the left of the check number.
