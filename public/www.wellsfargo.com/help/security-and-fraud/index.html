You have questions, we have answers

Top Questions

How can I protect myself from fraud?

Learn how to recognize common scams and cyber threats and review our fraud prevention tips to help protect your account and personal information.

What should I do if I lose my device or my device is stolen?

In addition to notifying your mobile carrier, please call 1-800-869-3557, 24 hours a day, to notify us that your device is lost or stolen. We can disable your biometric sign on (Touch ID®, Face ID®, fingerprint or facial recognition) feature, digital wallet, or FastLook service on your Wells Fargo Mobile app.

If you have the Find My iPhone (for Apple® devices) or Find My Device (for AndroidTM devices) app downloaded to your device, you can use it to remotely lock your device. Please refer to the manufacturer's website for more information.

How can I change my privacy preferences (sharing and receiving information)?

You can change your privacy preferences online or call 1-888-528-8460.

What is Voice Verification?

Voice Verification is a biometrics service that allows customers to use their unique voice to verify their identity when they call Wells Fargo for service on their accounts. Customers can choose to speak a simple passphrase instead of entering other information.

How does Wells Fargo help protect me from fraud?

We are consistently enhancing our security measures and identifying new and emerging threats to help keep your accounts and information secure. Learn how we identify you and help protect your data.
