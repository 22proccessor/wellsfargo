Get started investing in minutes

  1. Sign on to your Wells Fargo account to answer a few questions about your investment goals, appetite for risk, and timeframe.
  2. View your recommended investment portfolio – based on your responses to our questions.
  3. Open an online account.
  4. Sign your account documents and fund your account with at least $5,000 within 180 days.
