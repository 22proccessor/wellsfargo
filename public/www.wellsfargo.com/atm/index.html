Our network of approximately 12,000 ATMs makes banking easy and convenient

Digital wallet access

Add your Wells Fargo Debit Card to your digital wallet to easily access your accounts using your mobile device at any Wells Fargo ATM. Tap your digital wallet near the Contactless Symbol on the ATM and enter your PIN.

Tap your debit card

Tap your Wells Fargo contactless debit card near the Contactless Symbol and enter your PIN to quickly access any Wells Fargo ATM. Your card never leaves your hand.

Streamline deposits

Making deposits is easy. The ATM scans your checks, counts your bills, and totals them on the screen. Insert up to 30 checks and bills combined, and the ATM does the rest.

Make payments and transfers

Conveniently transfer funds between your personal or business accounts. You can also make payments to your Wells Fargo Credit Card.

Talking ATMs

Enhanced ATM access for customers who are blind or have vision impairments. Learn more about Talking ATMs.
